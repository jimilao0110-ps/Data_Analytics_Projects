# E-commerce Sales Performance Analysis & Interactive Dashboard
## Project Objective
The main goal of this project is to analyze global e-commerce sales data to understand business performance, identify sales trends, evaluate profitability across regions, track product demand, and measure delivery efficiency. The objective is to transform raw transactional data into an interactive dashboard that supports data-driven decisions, highlights growth opportunities, and pinpoints operational gaps.
## Dataset Used
[E-commerce Sales Dataset](https://github.com/jimilao0110-ps/Data_Analytics_Projects/blob/main/Ecommerce_Sales_Dashboard-Excel/E-com_Sales.xlsx)
## Questions / Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
This analysis answers the following business questions and tracks these core metrics:
1. **Overall Performance**

    - **Total Revenue:** $5,284,387.70

    - **Total Profit:** $1,437,638.31

    - **Overall Profit Margin:** 27%

    - **Total Customers:** 10,000

2. **Sales Trends:**

    - How do sales and profit change monthly and yearly?

    - What seasonal patterns exist in customer demand?

3. **Regional & Country Performance:**

    - Which countries and regions generate the highest revenue and profit?

    - How does performance differ between markets?

4. **Product Performance:**

    - How many units are sold each month?

    - What is the total volume of products sold (Grand Total: **896 units**)?

5. **Operational Efficiency:**

    - What percentage of orders are delivered on time? (**63% vs. 75% target**).

    - Breakdown of order status: Delivered, Cancelled, Processing, Returned.

## Process

1. **Data Collection & Loading**: Imported raw transaction data into Microsoft Excel.

2. **Data Cleaning & Preparation**:

    - Removed duplicates, fixed inconsistent values, handled missing data.

    - Standardized formats for dates, currency, and location names.

    - Created new calculated fields: Profit Margin %, Month/Year, Region Groups.

    - Used **Power Query** to transform and structure data for analysis.

3. **Data Modeling**: Organized tables and built relationships for accurate aggregation.

4. **Analysis & Calculation**: Used Pivot Tables and formulas to compute totals, averages, percentages, and comparisons.

5. **Visualization Design**: Designed charts and visuals to clearly present trends, comparisons, and KPIs.

3. **Dashboard Development**: Built interactive interface with **slicers** for Year and Country filtering.

7. **Testing & Validation**: Verified accuracy of calculations and ensured all filters work correctly.

## Project Insight
1. **Seasonal Pattern**: Sales peak significantly in **Q4 (October–December)** due to holiday demand, while January–February show the lowest performance.

2. **Regional Leaders**:

    - **Asia** generates the highest revenue ($1.33M).

    - **Middle East** delivers the strongest profit margins.

    - **North America** and **Europe** are stable, consistent contributors.
   
3. **Product Demand**: Sales volume grows steadily throughout the year, with a sharp increase from September onward; total 896 units sold across the period.

4. **Profit Health**: 27% overall margin is healthy, but profitability varies greatly by country and product type.
5. **Operational Gap**: On-time delivery rate is only **63%**, well below the 75% target — delays are a major issue affecting customer experience and cost.

## Final Conclusion

This project successfully transformed raw data into a clear, actionable view of the business. The analysis confirms strong revenue and profit performance, with clear seasonal and regional patterns that can be leveraged for growth. However, the low on-time delivery rate is a critical operational weakness that must be addressed.
